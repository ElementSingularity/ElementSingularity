Use Cases for drones

1. Delivery: these drones can be used for short-distance deliveries.
2. Military: the military mostly use drones for spying and recon and sometimes even air-strikes.
3. Emergency Rescue: in more severe/dangerous situations, it's better to send in a drone to rescue.
4. Agriculture: many farmers use drones for a variety of tasks to reduce physical strain, like seeding crops.
5. Wildlife Conservation: it's easier to track animal populations with an aerial view. It's also easier to drop a seed package to deforested land.
6. Historical Conservation: drones are sent in to make a 3D map of historical sites, like Chernobyl.
